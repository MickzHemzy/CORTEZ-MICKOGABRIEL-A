What is Aviation Information System?

The Bachelor of Science in Aviation Information Systems (BSAIS) with Specialization in Project Management is a four-year program that prepares students to be IT professionals with a solid background in both Information Technology and business concepts and applications related to the aviation industry. Students are trained to be expert in the design and implementation of computing solutions that integrate IT with business processes.

What does I.T. or I.S. Professionals do?

  • Analyzing technical problems and identifying computer requirements to solve those problems with appropriate IT solutions.
  • Designing, implementing and evaluating computer-based systems, processes, components, and programs.
  • Identifying, analyzing, and responding to user needs for the selection, creation, evaluation, and administration of computer-based systems.
